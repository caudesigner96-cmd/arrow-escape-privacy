Privacy Policy

App: Arrow Escape Neon
Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

Arrow Escape Neon collects the following data to provide and improve the game experience:

  • Anonymous user ID – automatically created when you first launch the game, used to save your progress.
  • Google account info (optional) – if you choose to sign in with Google, we collect your Google account email and display name to link your save data.
  • Game progress data – current level, completed levels, daily streaks, hints, and in-game settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save and restore your game progress across devices.
  • To provide daily puzzle levels and track streaks.
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) – for authentication and cloud data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob – for displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In – optional login to sync progress. Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage

All game data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties outside of the services listed above.

5. Advertising

Arrow Escape Neon displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

This game is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your game data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
